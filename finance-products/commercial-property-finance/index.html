We are Here

Have a question?We are happy to help

How fast can I get a finance decision?

Once we have the key details, we start the lender search and keep the process clear from the start. More complex funding needs may take longer.

What happens after I apply?

A Stonebridge finance specialist reviews your requirement, checks suitable lender routes, and explains the next steps so you know what information is needed and why.

Which finance products can you help with?

We support business loans, asset finance, invoice finance, merchant cash advance, commercial property finance, bridging, development finance, and other specialist business funding routes.

How is this different from going direct to a lender?

We compare options across our lender panel and help match the finance structure to your business, cash flow, sector, timescale, and funding purpose.

Will applying affect my credit score?

Initial eligibility conversations are designed to understand your needs before any formal lender checks. If a credit search is required later, we will explain that before it happens.

Do you work with start-ups?

Yes, some lenders can support newer businesses depending on the finance type, trading history, security, projections, and director background.

Is there a limit to how much I can borrow?

Funding amounts depend on the product, lender appetite, business profile, affordability, and available security. Stonebridge can support requirements from smaller working capital needs through to larger facilities.

Are there any sectors you do not support?

Most mainstream UK business sectors can be considered. Some lenders may have restrictions, so we check your sector and funding purpose early in the process.

Still undecided?

You can start with a simple conversation. We will help you understand what may be possible before you commit to a full application.
